Skapid'or is your new favorite place to taste rustic cuisine

Nowhere in Vienna is the Orient as close as in Skapid'or. Maybe that is because the energy and pulse of life is similar to the markets of Marakesh or the shouks of Tel Aviv, both in the Vienna itself as well as within the walls of Skapid'or's restaurant. We feel at home surrounded by the sounds of the market stalls and the smells of the spices. And you can taste that in our dishes.

A healthy portion of indifference and debauchery is absolutely permitted at the Skapid'or. Celebrating life and enjoyment is what we do best here.
